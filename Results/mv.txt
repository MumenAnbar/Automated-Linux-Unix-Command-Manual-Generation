>mv
__________________

DESCRIPTION
       Rename SOURCE to DEST, or move SOURCE(s) to DIRECTORY.

__________________

Version History:    mv (GNU coreutils) 8.32
__________________

Example: 
	>mv File1.txt File2.txt
	File1.txt will be renamed to File2.txt

__________________

Related Commands:          mv,mv,
__________________
